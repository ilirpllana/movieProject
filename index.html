About Our App

Our app is created to keep the list of movies that you have watched and plan to watch. Sometimes you want to re-watch some movies but forget the name at that time you can search for it in the list. Some new movies might be released and you want to add it but you didn't have enough time and plan to watch later but you forget. Bookmarking such movies can help you remember which movie you wanted to watch.
